About Us

A Little Swiss Bakery With A Love For Sweet Moments

Handcrafted Desserts, Warm Coffee & Swiss Tradition

Maison du Sucre is a small Swiss bakery built around simple pleasures: fresh pastries, rich chocolate, warm coffee and a welcoming place to slow down.

Inspired by classic Swiss baking, every dessert is prepared with care, from delicate meringues and buttery tortes to smooth chocolate creations. Whether you visit for breakfast, an afternoon treat or a special celebration, our goal is to make every bite feel memorable.
